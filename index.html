Our Mission

Welcome to Dickie & Lyman Lawyers LLP. We are dedicated to delivering the highest standards of legal service. Our goal is to inform you about our firm, our services, and the areas of law in which we specialize.

Our Lawyers

S. David Lyman

David has practiced residential tenancy law, property tax law, government relations, commercial tenancy law and litigation with John Dickie since 1999. In association with John, David has become a trusted voice for landlords in Ottawa. David has represented many of the largest landlords in Ottawa as well as many other local and national landlords...

PRACTICE

David Lyman has practiced residential rent control, landlord and tenant law, and municipal property tax assessment law, and related litigation with John Dickie since December 1999. David and John formed their partnership in November 2003.

RESIDENTIAL TENANCY LAW

David is Vice-President of the Eastern Ontario Landlord Organization and a trusted voice for landlords in Ottawa. Since 1999, David has represented landlords in more than 70 rent review applications, allowing landlords to optimize rents on tens of thousands of rental units. In the Eastern Region, David is the most active lawyer in rent review applications, representing dozens of landlords in applications covering thousands of rental units. David has acted for landlords in all types of matters before the Ontario Rental Housing Tribunal, now known as the Landlord and Tenant Board.

With John Dickie, David co-authored Working with the Residential Tenancies Act, a legal textbook published by Emond-Mongomery Publications. (to order the book call Emond-Montgomery at 1-888-837-0815 or click here for an order form.)

PROPERTY TAX MATTERS

David has worked on property tax matters since 1999, including reviewing assessments, appealing assessments, negotiating with assessors, analyzing tax allocations, and reviewing capping and clawback calculations. In addition to representing clients in the property tax field, David has published articles and presented papers on property taxes and assessment.

COMMERCIAL TENANCY LAW

David acts for landlords, property managers, and certain tenants in commercial leasing matters, including lease negotiations, lease interpretation disputes, and tax recovery disputes.

GOVERNMENT RELATIONS

David has worked with John Dickie in representing multi-residential, commercial, and non-profit taxpayers in government relations around rent control, landlord and tenant law, property tax exemption claims, the vacancy rebate rules, the relative levels of property taxes, and other matters of importance to those who manage, rent, or own real property in Ontario and across Canada.

EDUCATION

  • Bar Admission Course, 1999, Called February 2000
  • University of Toronto, LL.B., 1998
  • York University, B.A. (political science), 1995 (Graduate with Merit)

MEMBERSHIPS & ASSOCIATIONS

  • Eastern Ontario Landlord Organization (EOLO)
  • Canadian Property Tax Association (CPTA)
  • County of Carleton Law Association (CCLA)
  • Building Owners & Managers Association of Ottawa (BOMA Ottawa)

PERSONAL INTERESTS

A proud father and husband, David aims to balance his work life with his family life. David attempts to maintain an active lifestyle, participating in a number of individual and team sports. Proud to be involved in community needs, David has been a co-chair of the Legal Division of the UJA since 2004.
